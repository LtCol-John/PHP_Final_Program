A committment to quality

Buhrmann Farms is committed to providing the best agricultural commodities that money can buy. Our selection of grains, beef, hay, and fruit grown with the least amount of man-made pesticides and fertilizers demonstrates our committment to the environment. All this, with an emphasis on flavor and product quality

Buhrmann Farms affiliates and partners

Buhrmann Farms is proud to be a part of the Sprague-Martell community, and has several local business interests. Jeff Hollman is our current sharecropper on row-crops, and Ben Huerengardt is cropping off the hay and pasture ground.
