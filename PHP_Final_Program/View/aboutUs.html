GrowRight Farms, About Us...

Grow Right Farms Founder

Johan Dietrich Buhrmann

Grow Right Farms is an environmentally friendly, local, family run operation that thinks consumers and producers need to close the gap between those who eat agricultural products, and those that raise them.

Grow Right Farms history

Grow Rigth Farms has a long and storied history connected to the Nebraska community. In January of 1867, right after the enactment of the Homestead Act, Johan Dietrich Buhrmann and his two sons Christian and Wilhelm braved the Nebraska winter and staked a claim in what is now known as Lancaster County. Over time, the number of Buhrmann's who had farms grew to five, and it was known that farming was in their blood. In the early years it wasn't outrageous to see buffalo, deer, pheasants, quail and the occasional indian.

Grow Right Farms Philosophy

Our philosophy of farming at Grow Right Farms is quite simple, to produce the highest quality agricultural products possible, while treating the land, wildlife, and community with respect. Grow Right Farms minimizes the use of commerical pesticides and herbicides, emphasizes mechanical control of weeds, and attempts to use natural fertilzers when ever possible. Grow Right Farms healthy respect for the land, wildlife, and our communities helps us identify with our consumers to bring them the very best in agricultural products.
